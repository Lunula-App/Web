Privacy first

Your health data is yours alone

Lunula is built so that data collection is architecturally impossible — not just against our policy.

Everything stays on your device

No backend servers, no accounts, no outbound connections. Your cycle data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

Encrypted backups

Exports use AES-256-GCM encryption with PBKDF2-SHA256 (210,000 iterations). Your passphrase never leaves your device — not even Lunula can read it.

No tracking, no sharing

Zero analytics SDKs, no ad identifiers, no crash reporters. Your data is never sold or shared with any third party, ever.

You are in control

Export, delete, or reset your data at any time. We have no retention policies because we have no servers to store anything on.

Built differently

Architecture as a privacy guarantee

Most apps say they protect your privacy. Lunula's architecture makes data collection technically impossible — there are no servers to send data to.
